Teaching

Awake, Watch, and Pray

August 12, 2025 • Endtime Glory

In this critical hour, the Lord is calling His people to awaken from spiritual slumber. Jesus repeatedly warned His disciples to watch and pray, not as a suggestion, but as a safeguard against deception and weariness.

Why Watchfulness Matters

Spiritual alertness keeps our hearts sensitive to the voice of the Holy Spirit. When watchfulness fades, compromise creeps in quietly. The enemy rarely attacks loudly — he distracts patiently.

“Watch and pray, lest you enter into temptation. The spirit indeed is willing, but the flesh is weak.” — Matthew 26:41

Prayer as a Lifestyle

Prayer is not merely an activity; it is a posture of dependence. A praying believer stays aligned with heaven’s priorities and remains anchored when shaking increases.

  • Daily time alone with God
  • Scripture-fed, Spirit-led prayer
  • Repentance and humility
  • Intercession for others

A Call to the Watchmen

The Lord is raising watchmen on the walls — men and women who refuse spiritual complacency and who cry out day and night for righteousness to prevail.

May we be found awake, faithful, and burning with oil in our lamps when the Bridegroom comes.
